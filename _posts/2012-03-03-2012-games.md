<p class="meta">3 Feb 2012 - Pleasant Grove, UT</p>

I played through a handful of games in 2011, so I guess you could say that I have too much time on my hands.  Ironically the amount of quality games that release every year has begun to outweight the amount of time I would like to invest in them.  I have become quite fickle about my game choices.  I perused the big game list over at [ign](http://games.ign.com/articles/119/1192614p1.html) and hand picked the games that I am excited about.  

In the interest of practicing my hand at writing again I may even do some reviews of the ones I play this year.  In the meantime I grabbed some of the trailers for my curated 2012 list.
